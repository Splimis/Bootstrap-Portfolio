About Me

I was born in St. Louis, Missouri and almost immediately moved to Lawrence, New York. Some time later I moved to Freehold, New Jersey and graduated Freehold Township Highschool. Upon graduation I attended college at Wagner in Staten Island, New York. After my third symester I dropped out, as there is no money in being a music major. I then attended Lincoln Technical Institue in Newark and graduated with a --- avarage. I have been working in the automotive field ever since.

My employment history is as follows. In highschool I was a music store clerk. During College and immediately following I worked as a grounds keeper and mechanic at St. Vladimir's Russian Orthodox Christian Cemetery. Following my graduation from Lincoln Tech I obtained a job at a small shop in Freehold. I later moved on to a Pepboys in Howell NJ. My last position was Lead Technician at a medical transportation company in Tinton Falls.
